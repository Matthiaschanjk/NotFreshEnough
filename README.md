## 🥢 What is this?
**NotFreshEnough** is a ruthless, Asian-parent-style GitHub repository roaster. You input a GitHub URL, and our "Auntie AI" scrapes your codebase to judge your commit history, documentation, and architecture. Your project is then compared to many others on GitHub, Devpost and Linkedin.

It generates a highly shareable **"State Report Card"** that mathematically proves why your cousin Jun Jie is still the favorite.

## ✨ Features
* **Ruthless Repo Scraping:** Powered by **TinyFish**, we dive into your file structure, `README.md`, and commit history to find your deepest flaws. **TinyFish** then searches for similar projects, returning the top 3 most similar projects.
* **Market Auntie AI:** We pass your terrible code into **OpenAI**, equipped with a custom system prompt engineered to maximize emotional damage.
* **The "Freshness" Meter:** A dynamic loading state that questions your life choices while the AI generates your roast.
* **The Wall of Shame:** A beautifully designed, Apple-esque minimalistic report card that delivers your C grade with maximum aesthetic impact.
* **The Family's Opinon:** tells you what Ah Gong thinks, what your Kor Kor recommends you to do and gives you an analysis on what your other cousins are doing.
